## QUICK START: 4 STEPS

### Step 1: Gather Inspection Report (30 minutes)

**Get anonymized NIR report and extract**:
- Problem type (water, fire, mould)
- Location (suburb, city, state)
- Property details (age, type, size)
- Damage extent (materials, area)
- Timeline (incident to discovery)
- Scope of works (what was needed)
- Outcome (results, timeline, cost)

### Step 2: Identify Content Opportunities (15 minutes)

**The report generates 5-7 content pieces**:

1. **Blog post** - Full case study (2000-3000 words)
   - Target: "[Problem] restoration in [City]"
   - Goal: Establish expertise
   - Audience: Homeowners, property managers

2. **Social media content** - 5-10 posts
   - Target: LinkedIn (professional), Instagram (visual), TikTok (quick tips)
   - Goal: Social proof, engagement
   - Audience: Platform followers

3. **Landing page** - If location-specific
   - Target: "[Problem] restoration in [Suburb]"
   - Goal: Lead conversion
   - Audience: People in that suburb with that problem

4. **Backlink opportunity** - Guest post idea
   - Target: Industry publication
   - Goal: High-quality backlink + brand mention
   - Audience: Industry editors

5. **Local SEO** - Google My Business update
   - Target: Local keywords
   - Goal: Local pack ranking
   - Audience: Local search users

6. **Content cluster** - Pillar + supporting pages
   - Target: Topic authority (broad keyword)
   - Goal: Dominate topic
   - Audience: All related searches

7. **Video script** - YouTube case study
   - Target: "[Problem] case study [Location]"
   - Goal: Authority + video content
   - Audience: YouTube searchers

### Step 3: Generate Content (45 minutes)

**Use AI to generate each piece**:

```

4. Video script (10-15 minutes)

Include:
- Real data points (anonymized)
- IICRC standard references
- Before/after structure
- Local SEO keywords
- Trust signals

```

### Step 4: Optimize & Publish (30 minutes)

**SEO optimization**:
- Keywords in title, H1, H2, first paragraph
- Meta description (155 chars)
- Alt text for images
- Internal links to related content

**Publishing**:
- Schedule blog post
- Queue social posts
- Update Google My Business
- Submit for indexing
