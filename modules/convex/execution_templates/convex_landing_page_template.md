## Template Structure

### 1. Hero Section (Above the Fold - Critical)

**Purpose**: Stop scrolling, hook attention, communicate value in 3 seconds

**Components**:
```
[HEADLINE - Outcome-Focused Hook]
"The [Adjective] [Solution] for [Specific Audience]"

Template: "[Unexpected insight] that [Core Outcome]"
- Example: "Why 90% of [industry] fail at [goal] (and how to actually win)"
- Example: "The only [solution] that [specific result]"

Copywriting Cues:
- Use pattern interrupts (unexpected truth)
- Include specificity (not "marketers" but "B2B SaaS marketers")
- Promise measurable outcome (not "better" but "35% better")
- Make competitor positioning obvious (what they claim vs. what you own)

[SUBHEADLINE - Clarify and De-Risk]
"For [specific audience segment] who [specific situation] so they can [specific outcome]"

Copywriting Cues:
- Removes ambiguity about who this is for
- Acknowledges customer's actual situation
- Connects to emotional payoff

[LEAD IMAGE / VIDEO - Visual Proof]
Requirements:
- Shows transformation (before/after ideal)
- Not stock photo (real customer or product screenshot)
- Demonstrates outcome visually
- 1080px wide minimum, high quality

[PRIMARY CTA - Action + Benefit]
"[Action Verb] + [Primary Benefit]"
- Example: "See Your Results in 7 Days" (not just "Start Free Trial")
- Example: "Close Your Next Deal in Minutes" (not just "Try It")
- Copywriting: Use benefit language, not feature language

Design Direction:
- Color: High contrast (primary brand color)
- Size: Large button (40-50px height minimum)
- Placement: Centered, whitespace around button
- Hover: Clear hover state
- Accessibility: Clear focus state
```

**Measurable Targets**:
- Headline clarity: User can explain value in <10 seconds
- Subheadline fit: User feels "this is for me" or "this isn't for me"
- Image quality: Images load <2 seconds, 85%+ above-fold
- CTA prominence: Button immediately scannable

## Copywriting Framework

**Core Copy Principles** (Compression Rules):

1. **Outcome-First Language**
   - Feature: "AI-powered lead scoring"
   - Outcome: "Close deals 35% faster"
   - Language: Customer benefits, not technology

2. **Specificity Everywhere**
   - Generic: "Save time"
   - Specific: "Save 10 hours per week of prospecting"
   - Rule: Numbers beat adjectives

3. **Customer Voice**
   - Salesy: "Leverage our innovative lead qualification"
   - Customer Voice: "Stop wasting time on dead leads"
   - Rule: Use customer's words, not marketing words

4. **Removal of Friction Words**
   - Heavy: "Potentially enable your team to maybe improve"
   - Light: "Close 35% more deals"
   - Rule: Every word must serve conversion
