## Design Specifications

### Common Standards Across All Logos

**File Format**: SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics)
- Width: 200px
- Height: 200px
- ViewBox: 0 0 200 200
- No text elements (pure iconography for universal application)

**Color Palette**: Professional B2B appropriate
- Primary colors chosen to reflect industry
- Opacity variations for depth
- Accent colors for visual interest

**Design Principles**:
- Clean, modern, minimalist aesthetic
- Professional appearance suitable for B2B context
- Industry-appropriate symbolism
- Australian business aesthetic (not flashy, reliable, trustworthy)
- Scalable to any size without quality loss

**Accessibility**:
- High contrast color combinations
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Recognizable at small sizes (favicon)
- Clear at large sizes (hero sections)
