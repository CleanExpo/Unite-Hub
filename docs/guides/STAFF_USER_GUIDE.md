## Quick Start

Welcome to Unite-Hub! This guide will help you get productive quickly.

### Your First 5 Minutes

1. **Log in** at `https://your-domain.com/login` with Google
2. **Check your dashboard** - see today's hot leads
3. **Review contacts** - browse your assigned contacts
4. **Send an email** - respond to a warm lead
5. **Log an activity** - record your interaction

## 2. Managing Contacts

### Finding Contacts

**Search Bar** (top of page):
- Search by name, email, or company
- Use filters: Status, Score, Tags, Owner

**Filters:**
```
Status: New ‚Üí Contacted ‚Üí Qualified ‚Üí Proposal ‚Üí Won/Lost
Score:  Cold (0-39) ‚Üí Cool (40-59) ‚Üí Warm (60-79) ‚Üí Hot (80-100)
```

### Contact Detail View

Click any contact to see:

| Tab | Information |
|-----|-------------|
| **Overview** | Name, email, phone, company, AI score |
| **Activity** | Email history, calls, meetings |
| **Notes** | Your notes and team comments |
| **AI Insights** | Sentiment, intent, recommendations |

### Adding Contacts

1. Click **"+ New Contact"**
2. Fill required fields:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Company (optional)
3. Add optional info:
   - Phone
   - Job title
   - Source (how they found you)
   - Tags
4. Click **"Save"**

### Editing Contacts

1. Open contact detail view
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Update fields
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Assigning Contacts

1. Select contact(s) using checkboxes
2. Click **"Assign"** in bulk actions
3. Choose team member
4. Confirm

## 3. Understanding AI Scores

### How Scoring Works

The AI analyzes each contact and assigns a 0-100 score:

| Score Range | Label | What It Means |
|-------------|-------|---------------|
| 80-100 | **Hot** üî• | Ready to buy, prioritize immediately |
| 60-79 | **Warm** ‚òÄÔ∏è | Interested, nurture carefully |
| 40-59 | **Cool** üå§Ô∏è | Some interest, needs education |
| 0-39 | **Cold** ‚ùÑÔ∏è | Not ready, long-term nurture |

### Score Factors

| Factor | Weight | What AI Looks At |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| Email Engagement | 40% | Opens, clicks, replies |
| Sentiment | 20% | Positive/negative tone in emails |
| Intent | 20% | Buying signals, urgency words |
| Job Title | 10% | Decision-maker level |
| Status | 10% | Pipeline progression |

### Using Scores Effectively

**Daily Routine:**
1. Start with Hot leads (80+)
2. Follow up Warm leads (60-79)
3. Batch process Cool leads
4. Auto-nurture Cold leads via campaigns

## 5. Logging Activities

### Activity Types

| Type | When to Use |
|------|-------------|
| **Call** | Phone conversation |
| **Meeting** | In-person or video meeting |
| **Email** | Auto-logged when sent from Unite-Hub |
| **Note** | General observations |
| **Task** | To-do item |

### Logging a Call

1. Open contact
2. Click **"Log Activity"**
3. Select **"Call"**
4. Fill in:
   - Duration
   - Outcome (Connected, Voicemail, No answer)
   - Notes
5. Save

### Creating Tasks

1. Click **"+ Task"**
2. Enter:
   - Task description
   - Due date
   - Priority
   - Related contact
3. Save

Tasks appear in your dashboard and calendar.

## 6. Campaigns

### Viewing Campaigns

Go to **Campaigns** in sidebar.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Not sent yet |
| **Scheduled** | Will send at set time |
| **Active** | Currently sending/dripping |
| **Paused** | Temporarily stopped |
| **Completed** | Finished |

### Campaign Performance

Click a campaign to see:
- Total recipients
- Open rate (industry avg: 20-25%)
- Click rate (industry avg: 2-5%)
- Unsubscribes
- Bounces

### Enrolling Contacts

To add contacts to a campaign:

1. Select contacts from list
2. Click **"Add to Campaign"**
3. Choose campaign
4. Confirm

## 9. Daily Workflow

### Morning Routine (15 min)

1. **Check Hot Leads** - Any new 80+ scores?
2. **Review Tasks** - What's due today?
3. **Check Opens** - Who opened yesterday's emails?
4. **Plan Calls** - Schedule your outreach

### During the Day

1. **Work Hot ‚Üí Warm ‚Üí Cool** - Priority order
2. **Log Everything** - Calls, meetings, notes
3. **Update Statuses** - Keep pipeline accurate
4. **Use Templates** - Save time on emails

### End of Day (5 min)

1. **Update Tasks** - Mark complete or reschedule
2. **Log Activities** - Any unlogged calls?
3. **Check Tomorrow** - Preview next day's tasks
