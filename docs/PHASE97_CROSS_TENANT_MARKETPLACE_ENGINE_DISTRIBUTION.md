# Phase 97: Cross-Tenant Marketplace & Engine Distribution Portal (CTMEDP)

## Overview

The Cross-Tenant Marketplace & Engine Distribution Portal (CTMEDP) allows tenants to purchase engines, regional capacity, add-ons, compliance packs, and AI workflows through a unified marketplace.

## Features

- Engine products with one-click enable
- Regional capacity expansion
- Compliance pack SKUs (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)
- Quota expansion purchases
- Auto-provisioning on purchase

## Integration

- TCPQEL for licensing management
- GRH-RAPE & EGCBI for compliance packs
- AGLBASE for regional capacity
