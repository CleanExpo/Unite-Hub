# Designer Evaluation Rubric

**Project:** Australian Tradie Illustrations (20 custom assets)
**Budget:** $1,500-2,500 AUD
**Timeline:** 3 weeks (Weeks 7-9)

---

## Scoring System

**Total Points:** 100
**Pass Threshold:** 70+ points

**Rating Scale:**
- 90-100: Exceptional (immediate hire)
- 80-89: Strong candidate (schedule interview)
- 70-79: Good candidate (backup option)
- Below 70: Pass

---

## Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Portfolio Fit (40 points)

#### 1.1 Style Match (15 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Modern, friendly, approachable (NOT corporate)
- Clean line art with selective color
- Consistent style across samples

**Scoring:**
- 15pts: Perfect style match (examples look like what we want)
- 10pts: Close style (minor adjustments needed)
- 5pts: Acceptable style (would require significant direction)
- 0pts: Style doesn't fit (too corporate, too cartoony, etc.)

**Red Flags:**
- Overly cartoony/clipart style
- Corporate stock imagery only
- Inconsistent style across portfolio

#### 1.2 Industry Experience (10 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Construction/trades industry work
- Service-based business illustrations
- Real-world scenarios (not abstract)

**Scoring:**
- 10pts: Direct trades/construction experience
- 7pts: Service industry experience (general)
- 4pts: B2C consumer experience
- 0pts: No relevant industry experience

**Bonus (+5pts):**
- Australian client work
- Tradie/blue-collar specific projects

#### 1.3 Portfolio Quality (10 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- 10+ high-quality samples
- Diverse illustration types (characters, scenarios, icons)
- Professional presentation

**Scoring:**
- 10pts: Extensive portfolio (20+ samples), well-organized
- 7pts: Good portfolio (10-15 samples), clear presentation
- 4pts: Limited portfolio (<10 samples), decent quality
- 0pts: Minimal portfolio or poor quality

#### 1.4 Consistency (5 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Can maintain consistent style across 20+ assets
- Examples show series/sets (not one-offs)

**Scoring:**
- 5pts: Portfolio shows clear style consistency across projects
- 3pts: Generally consistent with minor variations
- 0pts: Style varies significantly across samples

---

### 2. Australian Context Understanding (30 points)

#### 2.1 Australian Client Experience (15 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Previous work for Australian clients/brands
- Understanding of Australian culture and context
- Local references in portfolio

**Scoring:**
- 15pts: Multiple Australian clients, clear local context
- 10pts: Some Australian work or strong research demonstrated
- 5pts: No Australian work but willing to research
- 0pts: No Australian context, doesn't acknowledge importance

**Questions to Ask:**
- "Have you worked with Australian clients before?"
- "What do you know about Australian tradie culture?"
- "Can you give examples of Australian residential architecture styles?"

#### 2.2 Cultural Awareness (10 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Understands tradie/blue-collar culture
- Avoids stereotypes or overly polished imagery
- Can represent diversity authentically

**Scoring:**
- 10pts: Demonstrates clear understanding, provides examples
- 7pts: Shows awareness, asks good clarifying questions
- 4pts: Limited understanding but open to learning
- 0pts: Misunderstands audience or uses stereotypes

**Red Flags:**
- Uses American construction imagery
- Overly corporate/polished aesthetic
- Doesn't acknowledge cultural differences

#### 2.3 Research Approach (5 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Willing to research Australian context
- Asks clarifying questions
- Provides thoughtful feedback on brief

**Scoring:**
- 5pts: Proactive research, asks specific questions
- 3pts: Willing to research, asks general questions
- 0pts: No research mentioned, passive acceptance

---

### 3. Technical Skills (20 points)

#### 3.1 File Format Proficiency (10 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- SVG AND PNG delivery capability
- Optimized for web use (<200KB per file)
- Organized file structure

**Scoring:**
- 10pts: SVG primary, PNG backup, optimized, organized
- 7pts: Can deliver both formats, needs optimization guidance
- 4pts: Only one format offered
- 0pts: Unclear on technical requirements

**Required Confirmation:**
- "Can you deliver SVG files with transparent backgrounds?"
- "How do you optimize files for web use?"
- "Do you provide source files (AI/Figma)?"

#### 3.2 Source File Delivery (5 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Adobe Illustrator (.ai) OR Figma files
- Layered and organized
- Editable for future revisions

**Scoring:**
- 5pts: Source files included, well-organized layers
- 3pts: Source files available upon request
- 0pts: No source files or unclear

#### 3.3 Web Optimization Knowledge (5 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Understands web performance requirements
- Can optimize without sacrificing quality
- Knows when to use SVG vs PNG

**Scoring:**
- 5pts: Clear understanding, provides optimization examples
- 3pts: Basic understanding, willing to learn
- 0pts: No understanding or unclear

---

### 4. Timeline & Communication (10 points)

#### 4.1 Timeline Feasibility (5 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Can deliver 20 illustrations in 3 weeks
- Provides realistic milestones
- Has availability during Weeks 7-9

**Scoring:**
- 5pts: Confirms 3-week timeline with clear milestones
- 3pts: Can deliver but tight timeline
- 0pts: Cannot meet 3-week deadline

**Red Flags:**
- Vague timeline ("I can start whenever")
- Overpromises ("I can do it in 1 week")
- Unclear availability

#### 4.2 Communication Quality (5 points)
**What we're looking for:**
- Clear, professional communication
- Responsive to messages
- Asks clarifying questions

**Scoring:**
- 5pts: Excellent communication, prompt responses, thoughtful questions
- 3pts: Good communication, somewhat responsive
- 0pts: Poor communication or unresponsive

**Assessment:**
- Response time to initial outreach
- Quality of proposal (detailed vs generic)
- Professionalism in communication

- Portfolio has multiple AU clients

### Industry-Specific Bonus (+5)
- Has designed for tradie marketplaces (HiPages, ServiceSeeking, etc.)
- Worked with construction/trades companies

## Interview Questions (Top 3 Candidates)

### Question Set 1: Style & Approach
1. "Walk me through your process for creating a new illustration set."
2. "How do you ensure consistency across 20+ assets?"
3. "Can you show me 3 different style variations for one illustration?"

**Looking for:**
- Clear process (concept → sketch → refine → final)
- Style guide creation
- Flexibility in approach

### Question Set 2: Australian Context
1. "What do you know about Australian tradie culture?"
2. "Can you describe the difference between Australian and American construction imagery?"
3. "What research would you do before starting this project?"

**Looking for:**
- Demonstrates understanding or willingness to learn
- Specific references (hi-vis, utes, WorkSafe standards)
- Proactive research approach

### Question Set 3: Project Logistics
1. "What's your revision policy?"
2. "How do you handle feedback/changes mid-project?"
3. "What happens if the project scope changes?"

**Looking for:**
- Clear revision policy (2 major rounds)
- Collaborative feedback process
- Transparent about scope changes

## Decision Criteria

### Immediate Hire (90+ points)
- Portfolio is perfect match
- Strong Australian context understanding
- Excellent communication
- Can start immediately

### Schedule Interview (80-89 points)
- Good portfolio fit with minor adjustments needed
- Some Australian experience or strong research approach
- Solid technical skills
- Available for 3-week timeline

### Backup Candidate (70-79 points)
- Acceptable style but needs direction
- Limited Australian context but willing to learn
- Meets technical requirements
- Available but not ideal timing

### Pass (Below 70 points)
- Style doesn't fit
- No Australian context and unwilling to research
- Missing technical skills
- Cannot meet timeline

---

## Contract Negotiation Points

### If Score: 90-100 (Premium Candidate)
**Budget:** Up to $2,500 AUD
**Why:** Perfect match, worth premium rate
**Terms:** Fixed price, 2 revision rounds, 30-40-30 payment schedule

### If Score: 80-89 (Strong Candidate)
**Budget:** $2,000 AUD
**Why:** Good match, standard rate
**Terms:** Fixed price OR hourly ($100-125/hr), 2 revision rounds

### If Score: 70-79 (Backup Candidate)
**Budget:** $1,500-1,750 AUD
**Why:** Acceptable but needs more direction
**Terms:** Milestone-based payments, 3 revision rounds (to account for learning curve)

## Red Flags (Immediate Disqualification)

- ❌ Cannot deliver within 3 weeks
- ❌ Portfolio shows no consistency
- ❌ Unwilling to research Australian context
- ❌ Cannot deliver SVG files
- ❌ Poor communication or unresponsive
- ❌ Plagiarism or stock imagery in portfolio
- ❌ Refuses to provide source files
- ❌ Unclear on revision policy

## Post-Selection Checklist

### After Designer Selected
- [ ] Send formal contract (terms, deliverables, payment schedule)
- [ ] Share detailed brief (`PHASE3_DESIGNER_BRIEF.md`)
- [ ] Provide brand guidelines (colors, typography)
- [ ] Schedule Week 7 kickoff call (Monday)

- [ ] Share reference materials (HiPages, Australian tradie examples)

### Week 7: Style Exploration
- [ ] Review 3 style variations (due Wednesday)
- [ ] Select final style direction (due Friday)
- [ ] Approve first illustration (reference for remaining 19)

### Week 8: Production Batch 1
- [ ] Review first 12 illustrations (due Wednesday)
- [ ] Provide feedback (same day)
- [ ] Approve revised batch (due Friday)

### Week 9: Production Batch 2 + Final Delivery
- [ ] Review remaining 8 illustrations (due Wednesday)
- [ ] Final revisions (due Thursday)
- [ ] Complete delivery: 20 SVG + PNG + source files (due Friday)
