# Content Calendar - User Guide

## Quick Start

The Content Calendar is your AI-powered social media planning assistant. Generate 30 days (Starter) or 90 days (Professional) of strategic, platform-optimized posts in seconds.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Navigate to Content Calendar
From your dashboard, click on **"Content Calendar"** in the main navigation.

### Step 2: Generate Your First Calendar

1. Click the **"Generate Calendar"** button
2. The AI will analyze:
   - Your customer persona
   - Your marketing strategy
   - Your content pillars
   - Platform best practices
3. Within 30-60 seconds, you'll have a complete calendar

### Step 3: Review and Approve Posts

Browse your calendar in two views:
- **Calendar View**: Visual monthly grid showing all posts
- **List View**: Detailed list with full post content

## Working with Your Calendar

### Approving Posts

1. Click on any post to view details
2. Review copy, hashtags, and image prompt
3. Click **"Approve"** when ready
4. Status changes to "Approved"

### Editing Posts

1. Open post details
2. Click **"Edit Post"**
3. Modify:
   - Post copy
   - Hashtags
   - Image prompt
   - Call-to-action
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Regenerating Posts

Not happy with a post? Regenerate it:

1. Click **"Regenerate with AI"**
2. AI creates a fresh version
3. Same strategic intent, new content
4. Review and approve

## Troubleshooting

### Calendar Won't Generate
**Problem**: Error when clicking "Generate Calendar"

**Solutions**:
- Ensure you have an active persona
- Verify marketing strategy exists
- Check tier limits (30 vs 90 days)
- Try smaller date range

### Post Content Not Relevant
**Problem**: AI posts don't match your business

**Solutions**:
- Update persona with more detail
- Refine marketing strategy
- Edit and regenerate individual posts
- Provide feedback to improve future generations

### Engagement Metrics Missing
**Problem**: Can't see performance data

**Solutions**:
- Manually update engagement after publishing
- Ensure posts marked as "Published"
- Wait for metrics to populate
- Check platform analytics

### Can't Edit Post
**Problem**: Edit button not working

**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors
- Try different browser
- Contact support
