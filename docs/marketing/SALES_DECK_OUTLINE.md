## Presentation Tips

### Before the Meeting
- Research the prospect's business
- Check their website for context
- Know which tier they likely fit
- Prepare relevant case study

### During the Presentation
- Ask questions in first 2 minutes
- Customize examples to their business
- Skip slides that aren't relevant
- Demo if possible
- End with clear next step

### Follow-Up
- Send summary email within 24 hours
- Include relevant case study
- Offer trial link
- Schedule next call
