## FAQs

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?**
A: Yes, change plans anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my contact limit?**
A: You'll be prompted to upgrade or purchase additional contacts. Existing contacts remain accessible.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes, 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required to start.

**Q: What's included in onboarding?**
A: Professional gets a 30-min call. Elite gets 2 hours + dedicated CSM + custom training.

**Q: Can I get a custom plan?**
A: For 10,000+ contacts or enterprise needs, contact sales@synthex.com.au for custom pricing.
