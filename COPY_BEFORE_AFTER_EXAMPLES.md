# Copy Rewrite: Before & After Examples

**Date**: December 2, 2025
**Project**: Unite-Hub Phase 2 Polish
**Audience**: Australian tradie/service business owners (35-55)

## Purpose of This Document

This document shows side-by-side comparisons of old vs. new copy to demonstrate how we've:
1. Eliminated corporate jargon
2. Made benefits crystal clear
3. Used plain English that tradies understand
4. Added personality and empathy
5. Created specific, actionable messaging

**Share this with stakeholders** to get buy-in on the new voice.

## AUDIENCE ALIGNMENT

Every piece of copy asks: **"Would a 45-year-old plumber understand this?"**

### We Use:
- ‚úÖ "callbacks" (not "conversions")
- ‚úÖ "jobs" (not "revenue opportunities")
- ‚úÖ "on the job site" (not "in the field")
- ‚úÖ "quotes" (not "proposals" or "estimates")
- ‚úÖ Plain English (no corporate buzzwords)

### We Avoid:
- ‚ùå "Leverage", "utilize", "synergize"
- ‚ùå "Solution", "platform", "ecosystem"
- ‚ùå "Actionable insights", "paradigm shift"
- ‚ùå "Transform", "disrupt", "innovate"
