## Key Products & Systems

### Nexus CRM (Unite-Group)
- **Purpose:** Central orchestration hub — manages all 7 businesses
- **Stack:** Next.js 15, Supabase, TypeScript, Vercel
- **Status:** Active — Nexus 2.0 rebuild complete (March 2026)
- **Key features:** MACAS advisory, Synthex A/B engine, contact management, approval queue

### MACAS (Multi-Agent Competitive Accounting System)
- **Purpose:** 4 competing AI accounting firms debate to produce better financial advice
- **Firms:** 4 parallel firms with distinct methodologies
- **Strategy:** `Promise.allSettled` — min 2 firms required; 1s/2s/4s backoff

- **Use:** Generates competing content variants; measures performance
