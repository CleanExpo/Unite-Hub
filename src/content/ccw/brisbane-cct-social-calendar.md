## Post 7 — Authority / Tips (LinkedIn)
**Date**: Friday 20/03/2026
**Type**: Expert tips / value post

5 things every commercial cleaning technician should know before their first big contract:

1. Pre-inspection documentation protects you. Photograph and note every existing stain, mark, and damage point before you start. This is your evidence if a client claims you caused damage.

2. pH matters more than brand. Understanding where your cleaning agent sits on the pH scale — and why — prevents fibre damage, colour bleeding, and surface etching.

3. Drying time is your reputation. Over-wet carpet leads to mould, odour, and callbacks. Measure, don't guess.

4. Your scope-of-works is a contract. Write it clearly, get it signed, and stick to it. Scope creep kills margins.

5. Certification isn't optional anymore. IICRC CCT is the baseline that insurers and property managers expect. Without it, you're invisible to the best contracts.
