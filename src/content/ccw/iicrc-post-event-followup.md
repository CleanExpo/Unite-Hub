G'day [FIRST_NAME],

Congratulations on completing the IICRC [COURSE_NAME] course and passing your examination.

Your IICRC certification is attached to this email. You'll also receive your physical certification card from IICRC directly within 4–6 weeks.

Here's what to do next:

1. **Verify your listing** — Check that your name appears on the IICRC Certified Technician Registry at iicrc.org. If it doesn't appear within 5 business days, let us know and we'll follow up with IICRC on your behalf.
