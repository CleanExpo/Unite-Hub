# CCW Imagen 4 / Midjourney Prompt Library

8 production-ready prompts for CCW marketing visuals. Each prompt is optimised for photorealistic corporate imagery suitable for website, LinkedIn, brochures, and pitch decks.

**Global style notes:** All images should convey professionalism, competence, and trustworthiness. Clean compositions, natural lighting where possible, no stock-photo cliches (no pointing at screens, no handshakes, no thumbs up). Australian settings.

---

## Prompt 1: Restoration Team Entering Flooded Commercial Space

**Prompt:**
Photorealistic wide-angle photograph of a 4-person restoration team entering a flooded commercial office space. Team members wear full PPE: white Tyvek coveralls, P2 respirators around necks, blue nitrile gloves, steel-cap rubber boots. They carry professional equipment — moisture meters, a clipboard, LED work lights. The office has 50mm of standing water on carpet tiles, overturned desk chairs, and waterlogged ceiling tiles sagging. Natural light from large commercial windows, supplemented by the team's work lights creating warm pools of illumination. Shot from slightly low angle to convey authority and competence. Muted tones with blue-grey colour palette. Australian commercial office interior. Corporate documentary photography style.

**Negative prompts:** Cartoon, illustration, oversaturated colours, stock photo poses, smiling at camera, unrealistic lighting, CGI look, blurry, low resolution, text overlays, watermarks.

**Aspect ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** Photorealistic, corporate documentary

Shot from the technician's shoulder height. Professional, competent, focused expression. Australian interior.

## Usage Notes

- All prompts are written for **Imagen 4** and **Midjourney v6+** compatibility
- For Imagen 4: paste the full prompt into the text field; negative prompts can be appended after "Avoid:" or used in the negative prompt field if available
- For Midjourney: append `--style raw --v 6` and convert negative prompts to `--no` parameters
- Adjust aspect ratios using `--ar` in Midjourney or the aspect ratio selector in Imagen
- Post-production: add CCW branding, text overlays, and colour grading to match the Scientific Luxury design system (#050505 backgrounds for dark layouts, #00F5FF cyan accents)
- All images should be reviewed for Australian context accuracy before publishing
