At 2am on a Tuesday, a property manager calls about a burst pipe flooding a 12-unit apartment complex.

Your generic CRM? It's designed for 9-to-5 sales pipelines. It doesn't know what an IICRC scope-of-works looks like. It can't auto-dispatch the nearest certified crew. It definitely can't generate an insurance-ready moisture report.

So you open a spreadsheet instead. Again.

We've run restoration businesses across Australia for over a decade. We've used Salesforce, HubSpot, ServiceM8, and half a dozen industry-specific tools. None of them understood the workflow: emergency call → triage → dispatch → drying management → insurer liaison → final sign-off.

So we built one that does.

Unite-Group's CRM is purpose-built for the restoration and cleaning industry. It handles:

- **24/7 job intake** with automated triage and crew dispatch
- **Insurance claim tracking** with document generation and insurer comms
- **Drying logs and moisture monitoring** integrated into the job timeline
- **Multi-business management** for operators running several brands under one roof

We're not a Silicon Valley startup guessing what tradies need. We're restoration operators who got tired of duct-taping generic software together.

**Built in Australia. For Australian restoration businesses.**
