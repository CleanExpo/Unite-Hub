# How to Access the CRM

## The CRM is located at: `/dashboard/crm`

## Step-by-Step Access:

1. **First, make sure you've run the role fix:**
   - Run `FIX_ROLE_CASE.sql` in Supabase to fix your role capitalization

2. **Login at the main site:**
   - Go to your app URL
   - Click "Login" in the navigation
   - Use email: `phill.m@carsi.com.au`
   - Use your password

3. **Navigate to CRM:**
   - After login, you'll be taken to `/dashboard`
   - Look for the **"CRM Dashboard"** button on the main dashboard
   - Click it to access `/dashboard/crm`

## Alternative Direct Access:

Once logged in, you can directly go to:
- `https://your-app-url.vercel.app/dashboard/crm`

## CRM Features Available:

- Overview tab with metrics
- Client management
- Deal pipeline
- Tasks and activities
- Data quality tools
- Business insights

## Troubleshooting:

If you can't access the CRM:

1. **Check your login status** - Are you logged in?
2. **Check the URL** - Make sure you're at `/dashboard/crm`
3. **Check browser console** - Any error messages?
4. **Clear cache** - Try incognito mode

## Quick Links:

- Main Dashboard: `/dashboard`
- CRM Dashboard: `/dashboard/crm`
- Login Page: `/login`
- Logout: Click "Logout" in navigation
