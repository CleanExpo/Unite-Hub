**"We already have a CRM"**
> "Great! We integrate with Salesforce and HubSpot. Most customers use us for the AI features and sync to their existing CRM for reporting."

**"AI-generated content sounds impersonal"**
> "That's the difference - we use Claude Opus 4 with Extended Thinking. It analyzes the contact's history and writes custom messages, not templates. 95%+ quality rating in our tests."

**"How much does the AI cost?"**
> "AI costs are included in your plan. We've optimized with prompt caching to keep it predictable. No surprise bills."

**"Is my data secure?"**
> "Yes. Encrypted in transit and at rest. SOC 2 Type II certified (in progress). Row Level Security means you can only see your own data. We never train on your data."

**"What if I need help?"**
> "Professional plan includes email support (24hr response). Enterprise gets dedicated Slack channel and priority support."
